# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

# Do’s
- Think before posting — assume everything you post is permanent
- Use respectful and professional language
- Verify facts before sharing any information
- Adjust privacy settings to control who sees your content
- Share positive and meaningful content (projects, achievements, skills)
# Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
- Don’t spread misinformation or rumors
- Don’t engage in unnecessary online arguments or trolling
- Don’t post impulsively when emotional or angry
