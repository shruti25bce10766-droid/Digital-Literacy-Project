# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

# General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Do not click on suspicious links or download files from unknown sources
- Always verify the sender before responding to emails, messages, or calls
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or personal accounts
- Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities
- Limit the personal information you share on social media
# Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone — even if they claim to be from a bank
- Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN or approving a request
- Always double-check payment requests before approving them
- Use only official and trusted payment apps for transactions
# Reporting & Action
- Immediately report cyber fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud reporting
- Inform your bank immediately if you suspect unauthorized transactions
